Who are we?

We are a family from Mérida México, who loves to make cakes and different kinds of desserts. Our neighbors and friends loved to try our cakes so one day we decided to make something bigger. We want to share with others our original home made taste for a good price and in the confort of your house. We are committed to give you the best tasting experience to enjoy with family and friends.

Why Sweet Sweet Home is better than others?

Well, we have the best quality and prices in the internet and in the zone. We offer you a great variety of what we do, you can tell us what desserts you need and we can make them for you.

What is about health orders?

We understand that everyone has different needs, so we have our special line of less calories and sugar cakes for those who wants to enjoy sweet treats but at the same time wants to care for their health and body.

How can I order?

It’s easy!

  • Think about what you want.
  • Go to our website.
  • Choose what you need.
  • Call us or contact us here in our page.
  • Tell us what you want and how many of them.
  • We will make your order.

You can pay when your order is delivered to the comfort of your home.
