Target Audience


The target audience of this website is men & women locals or tourists. Age: 18-60 approx. Income: $0-$15,000 (or above). Education: High School or higher. Status: Single, Married, other.

Personas:

Joseph: He is a retired 52 years old man from California. He loves visit other states in his own country with his wife, he is about to celebrate his 30 anniversary so he is looking for a peaceful and beautiful place in Idaho, so he is looking for pages that let him know how is the weather in that state to know if by the time he will go all will be perfect and to choose the most beautiful place to surprise his wife.

Pedro: He is a college young man originary from Mexico who is studying at Utah. He and his friends are planning visit Idaho this year by summer but they don't want to waste his visit, so first want to go to Soda Spring and then to Fish Heaven. They want to go Idaho when the temperature isn't too cold because Pedro has asthma and he needs to care for it, so he is looking for trustworthy pages that let him know all about weather in thoses places.


Scenarios

  • It has been raining a lot this week. Would it be safe go to hiking? If not, what else can we do?
  • It's too hot today, where can we go to swim to refresh?
  • My doggie and I want to go to run, where is the closest park?
  • My kids are imperatives, so I'm looking for the most safe outdoor activity where to take them. What do you recommend me?
  • We want to go to ride a bike in a place with beautiful landscapes, where we can go?
