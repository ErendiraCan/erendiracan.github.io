Target Audience


The target audience of this website is men & women locals or tourists. Age: 18-60 approx. Income: $0-$15,000 (or above). Education: High School or higher. Status: Single, Married, other.

Personas:

Joseph: He is a retired 52 years old man originary from Canada. He loves visit other countries with his wife, he is about to celebrate his 30 anniversary in a beautiful beach in the Caribbean so he is looking for pages that let him know how is the weather in those places to know if by the time he will go all will be perfect and to choose the most beautiful beach to surprise his wife.

Luca: He is a college young man originary from Italy. He and his friends are planning visit Mexico this year but they don't want to waste his visit so first want to go to the Mayan beaches. They want to go Mexico when the temperature isn't too hot and the UV rays won't be too dangerous because Luca has a skin problem and he needs to care for it, so he is looking for trustworthy pages that let him know all about weather in thoses places.


Scenarios

  • It has been raining a lot this week. Would it be safe go to the mangroves? If not, what else can we do?
  • It's too hot today, where can we go to swim to refresh?
  • My doggie and I want to go to the beach, where is the closest a dog friendly beach?
  • My kids are imperatives, so I'm looking for the most safe outdoor activity where to take them. What do you recommend me?
  • We want to go to ride a bike in a place where there won't be cars, where we can go?
